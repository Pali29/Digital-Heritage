About Tripura

Cultural Diversity

Tripura is known for its diverse ethnic communities, each contributing to the state's rich cultural tapestry.

Historical Sites

The state is home to several ancient temples and historical sites that reflect its historical significance.

Natural Beauty

From lush green hills to serene lakes, Tripura’s natural landscapes are breathtaking and serene.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Tripura is renowned for its traditional handicrafts, including bamboo and cane work, and weaving.

Dance & Music

Traditional dance forms like the Manipuri dance and folk music are integral to Tripura’s cultural identity.

Languages

The primary languages spoken are Bengali and Kokborok, reflecting the state’s diverse linguistic heritage.

Festivals of Tripura

Durga Puja

A major festival celebrated with grandeur, showcasing elaborate decorations and rituals.

Kharchi Puja

Buisu Festival

A harvest festival celebrated by the Tripuri people, marked by traditional rituals and community feasting.
