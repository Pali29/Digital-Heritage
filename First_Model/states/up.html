About Uttar Pradesh

Historical Landmarks

Uttar Pradesh is home to iconic landmarks such as the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Fatehpur Sikri.

Diverse Culture

The state boasts a rich cultural heritage with traditional festivals, music, and dance.

Spiritual Significance

Uttar Pradesh is a significant spiritual center with important sites such as Varanasi and Mathura.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Uttar Pradesh is known for its traditional art forms like Chikan embroidery, pottery, and brassware.

Dance & Music

Enjoy classical dance forms like Kathak and traditional music performances from the region.

Languages

The principal languages are Hindi and Urdu, with various regional dialects spoken across the state.

Festivals of Uttar Pradesh

Kumbh Mela

An important pilgrimage and festival, attracting millions of devotees to the banks of sacred rivers.

Diwali

The festival of lights is celebrated with great fervor across Uttar Pradesh, with elaborate decorations and festivities.

Holi

A vibrant festival of colors, celebrating the arrival of spring with joyous gatherings and color play.
