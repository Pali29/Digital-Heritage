About Meghalaya

Stunning Landscapes

Meghalaya is known for its scenic beauty, including lush green hills, waterfalls, and unique living root bridges.

Rich Culture

The state is home to diverse indigenous tribes with distinct customs, languages, and festivals.

Historical Significance

Meghalaya has a rich history reflected in its ancient traditions and historical sites.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Meghalaya is known for its traditional weaving, bamboo craft, and intricate hand-woven textiles.

Dance & Music

The state celebrates various traditional dance forms and music, including the famous Shad Suk Mynsiem and Wangala dance.

Languages

The main languages include Khasi, Jaintia, and Garo, each with its own unique linguistic characteristics.
