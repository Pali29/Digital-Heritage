About Sikkim

Natural Beauty

Sikkim is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, including the majestic Kanchenjunga, the highest peak in India.

Cultural Richness

The state boasts a rich cultural tapestry with influences from Tibetan, Nepali, and Bhutanese cultures.

Ecological Diversity

Sikkim’s diverse flora and fauna make it a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Sikkim is known for its traditional Thangka paintings and intricate wood carvings.

Dance & Music

Traditional dance forms like Mask Dance and folk music reflect the rich cultural heritage of Sikkim.

Languages

Sikkim’s linguistic diversity includes languages like Nepali, Bhutia, and Lepcha.

Festivals of Sikkim

Buddha Jayanti

A significant festival celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Buddha.

Losar

Tibetan New Year celebrated with traditional dances, music, and feasts.

Doh-Kho

A festival marking the end of the harvest season, celebrated with various traditional events.
