Festivals of Bihar

Sonepur Mela

One of Asia's largest cattle fairs, held annually in Sonepur, featuring various cultural and commercial activities.

Chhath Puja

A significant festival dedicated to the Sun God, celebrated with fervor in Bihar through rituals and offerings.

Buddha Jayanti

Celebrates the birth, enlightenment, and death of Buddha, with events and rituals at Buddhist sites like Bodh Gaya.
