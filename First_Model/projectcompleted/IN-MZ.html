About Mizoram

Natural Beauty

Mizoram is renowned for its lush green hills, scenic landscapes, and tranquil environment.

Rich Culture

The state has a rich cultural heritage with traditional festivals, music, and dance.

Historical Significance

Mizoram’s history is reflected in its traditional practices and historical sites.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Traditional weaving and bamboo craft are significant aspects of Mizoram’s artistic heritage.

Dance & Music

Mizoram is known for its traditional dance forms like Cheraw and Khuallam, and its folk music.

Languages

Mizo (Lusei) is the principal language, with various dialects spoken across the state.

Festivals of Mizoram

Chapchar Kut

A major festival marking the end of winter and the arrival of spring with traditional dances and feasts.

Thalfavang Kut

A harvest festival celebrating the end of the harvest season with traditional performances and feasts.

Pu Buai Festival

A significant festival dedicated to honoring ancestors and the spirits of nature.
