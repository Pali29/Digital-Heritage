About Jharkhand

Tribal Culture

Jharkhand is known for its diverse tribal culture, with unique traditions and practices.

Historical Significance

The state has a rich history with numerous ancient temples and historical sites.

Natural Wonders

From lush forests to scenic waterfalls, Jharkhand’s natural beauty is truly captivating.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Jharkhand is famous for its traditional arts and crafts, including dokra metal art and handwoven textiles.

Dance & Music

Traditional dance forms like Sohrai and Chhau, along with folk music, are integral to Jharkhand’s culture.

Languages

The primary languages spoken are Hindi, Santali, and Mundari, reflecting the state’s diverse linguistic heritage.

Festivals of Jharkhand

Sohrai Festival

A tribal festival celebrating the harvest with traditional rituals and community gatherings.

Chhath Puja

A major festival dedicated to the Sun God, celebrated with fasting, offerings, and rituals.

Jitiya

A festival observed by married women for the well-being and long life of their children.
