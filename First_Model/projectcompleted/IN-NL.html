About Nagaland

Tribal Diversity

Nagaland is known for its diverse tribal communities, each with its own distinct traditions and customs.

Rich Cultural Heritage

The state boasts a rich cultural heritage with traditional festivals, dances, and crafts.

Scenic Landscapes

From rolling hills to lush forests, Nagaland offers breathtaking natural landscapes and serene environments.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Nagaland is known for its intricate bamboo and cane crafts, as well as vibrant traditional textiles.

Dance & Music

Traditional dances like the Hornbill Dance and folk music are integral to Nagaland’s cultural celebrations.

Languages

The state is home to several languages and dialects, including Ao, Konyak, and Chakhesang.

Festivals of Nagaland

Hornbill Festival

Known as the "Festival of Festivals," it showcases the rich cultural heritage of Nagaland’s tribes.

Aoling Festival

A major festival of the Ao Naga tribe, celebrating the arrival of spring and the harvest season.

Sekrenyi Festival

Celebrated by the Angami tribe, it marks the end of the traditional harvest season with various rituals and feasts.
