Festivals of Arunachal Pradesh

Losar Festival

A major festival celebrated by the Monpa tribe, marking the Tibetan New Year with traditional ceremonies.

Hornbill Festival

A vibrant festival showcasing the cultural richness of the Naga tribes, celebrated with dance, music, and crafts.

Solung Festival

Observed by the Adi tribe, this festival involves traditional dances and offerings to the deities for a good harvest.
