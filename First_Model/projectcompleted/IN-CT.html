About Chhattisgarh

Cultural Diversity

Chhattisgarh is known for its diverse tribal cultures and unique traditions.

Historical Significance

The state has a rich history with ancient temples, forts, and monuments that reflect its historical importance.

Natural Beauty

Chhattisgarh’s landscape includes lush forests, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries, offering stunning natural beauty.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Chhattisgarh is renowned for its traditional art forms, including bell metal crafts and tribal paintings.

Dance & Music

Traditional dance forms like the Panthi and Raut Nacha, along with folk music, are integral to the state’s culture.

Languages

The primary language is Chhattisgarhi, with Hindi also widely spoken across the state.

Heritage Sites

Raigarh Fort

Kanha National Park

Bastar Palace

Festivals of Chhattisgarh

Hareli Festival

Celebrated to mark the beginning of the monsoon season, it includes rituals and agricultural activities.

Bastar Dussehra

A unique Dussehra festival celebrated with traditional rituals and processions in Bastar.

Chhath Puja

A major festival dedicated to the Sun God, observed with fasting, prayers, and offerings.
