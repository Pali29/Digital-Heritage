About Rajasthan

Historic Forts

Rajasthan is known for its majestic forts and palaces, including the iconic Mehrangarh Fort and Amber Fort.

Rich Culture

The state boasts a vibrant culture with traditional music, dance, and colorful festivals.

Desert Landscapes

Experience the unique desert landscapes of Rajasthan, including the famous Thar Desert.

Our Cultural Elements

Art & Craft

Rajasthan is renowned for its traditional art forms, including block printing, pottery, and intricate jewelry.

Dance & Music

Experience traditional dance forms like Ghoomar and Kalbeliya, and enjoy folk music performances.

Languages

The primary language is Hindi, with Rajasthani and various local dialects spoken across the state.

Festivals of Rajasthan

Desert Festival

A vibrant festival held in Jaisalmer featuring camel races, folk music, and dance performances.

Pushkar Fair

An annual cattle fair and pilgrimage site, famous for its colorful cultural activities and traditional events.

Teej

A festival celebrating the monsoon season with traditional dances, songs, and colorful attire.
